Vishoka Welfare Association works on the principle of "Towards a Life of Bliss", guiding people toward a happy, peaceful, and balanced life while serving humanity with love, compassion, and cooperation.

The Association is dedicated to the memory of the late Shri Ashok Kumar and continues to reduce suffering through free health camps, blood donation drives, food distribution, clothing donations, yoga camps, and health awareness programs.

Introduction | परिचय

Who We Are | हमारे बारे में

English

Vishoka Welfare Association is a dedicated social organization working continuously for the upliftment of society, based on the core values of service, compassion, and awareness. Under the inspiring leadership of Ms. Deepika Kapoor, the organization strives to bring positive change in people’s lives through wellness programs, healthcare services, awareness campaigns, and humanitarian initiatives.

Its objective is not only to provide support but also to empower individuals, promote self-reliance, and create a healthy, aware, and inclusive environment. Vishoka Welfare Association remains committed to serving humanity with love, empathy, and cooperation.

हिंदी

विषोका वेलफेयर एसोसिएशन एक समर्पित सामाजिक संस्था है, जो सेवा, करुणा और जागरूकता के मूल्यों पर आधारित होकर समाज के उत्थान के लिए निरंतर कार्य कर रही है। सुश्री दीपिका कपूर के प्रेरणादायक नेतृत्व में, यह संस्था वेलनेस प्रोग्राम, स्वास्थ्य सेवाओं, जागरूकता अभियानों और मानवीय कार्यों के माध्यम से लोगों के जीवन में सकारात्मक परिवर्तन लाने का प्रयास करती है।

हमारा उद्देश्य केवल सहायता प्रदान करना ही नहीं, बल्कि समाज के हर वर्ग को सशक्त बनाना, उन्हें आत्मनिर्भर बनाना और एक स्वस्थ, जागरूक एवं समावेशी वातावरण का निर्माण करना है। विषोका वेलफेयर एसोसिएशन प्रेम, सहानुभूति और सहयोग की भावना के साथ मानवता की सेवा के लिए प्रतिबद्ध है।

Mission | हमारा उद्देश्य

To serve humanity with compassion, dedication, and a spirit of selfless service. सेवा, करुणा और समर्पण के साथ मानवता की सेवा करना तथा समाज में स्वास्थ्य और जागरूकता को बढ़ावा देना।

Vision | हमारी दृष्टि

To build a healthy, aware, empowered, and inclusive society. एक स्वस्थ, जागरूक, सशक्त और समावेशी समाज का निर्माण करना।

Our Main Objectives

Serving Humanity

Helping the Needy

To help the needy people and reduce suffering from their lives by giving them a better and healthier life.

Free Health & Blood Camps

It organizes free health camps, consultations, free treatment support, and blood donation camps for the community.

Food Distribution

To provide food to the poor and ensure that no one in the community sleeps hungry.

Clothing Donations

It donates clothes to the needy and underprivileged, offering warmth, dignity, and support.

Yoga & Awareness

It conducts yoga and health awareness programs, helping reduce suffering and improving overall well-being.
